Our Clinets

We have built the infrastructure of the company around client needs, aligning ourselves with organizations when we find a good match and potential for growth. The company structure is central to who we are, an organization that is both big and nimble, a company organized to respond to our clients’ highest expectations of performance and service on projects of every type and size. At MostGroup, our goal is to keep our clients for life. We are a relationship focused organization that seeks long lasting relationships with our clients and industry associates.

About Us

Over the past 15 years, we have transformed ourselves from a successful building construction management firm into an innovative, forward-thinking organization that has big ambitions—to change the construction industry as we know it. In fact, MostGroup's vision is to transform the construction experience by consistently delivering the very best solutions, answers, and results on our projects.
In addition to that value, we have established a strong corporate culture that emphasizes innovation on our projects and in our operations. We embrace new practices, ideas, and ways of thinking, and our construction management teams are inquisitive, open-minded, and have a strong commitment to collaboration and open communication on our project sites.

Our History

Most Group KG

Kyrgyzstan's Premier Builder

Established in 1998, MostGroup Construction, LLC. is a specialized organization that performs the full range of work on construction and reconstruction, major repairs of automobile and railway bridges, tunnels, pipelines, hydraulic structures, the construction of industrial and civil purpose.
