ABOUT US

Over the past 15 years, we have transformed ourselves from a successful building construction management firm into an innovative, forward-thinking organization that has big ambitions—to change the construction industry as we know it. In fact, MostGroup's vision is to transform the construction experience by consistently delivering the very best solutions, answers, and results on our projects.
In addition to that value, we have established a strong corporate culture that emphasizes innovation on our projects and in our operations. We embrace new practices, ideas, and ways of thinking, and our construction management teams are inquisitive, open-minded, and have a strong commitment to collaboration and open communication on our project sites.

Our History

Most Group KG

Commitment to safety and quality

Established in 1998, MostGroup Construction, LLC. is a specialized organization that performs the full range of work on construction and reconstruction, major repairs of automobile and railway bridges, tunnels, pipelines, hydraulic structures, the construction of industrial and civil purpose. We operate under KRC-2 (first level of responsibility) license number 04504 as of June 26, 2012

Therefore, we strive to conduct our business sustainably and expand the breadth of our ethical business practices whether it is contnuous improvement of the level of quality, efficiency and safety across our operation, or, continuing and expanding our efforts in philathropy.
