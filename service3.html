Ready-mixed concrete

We currently have 3 plants (2 in Bishkek and 1 in Osh) allowing us a wide range of delivery areas. Our latest technology and a fleet of mixer trucks provide superior service alongside an excellent product. Most-Group has a diversified product list so whether you need a mix for paving, lightweight slabs or a pervious application, we are confident we can provide a reliable and cost effective mix. We invite you to come take a look at our products and to please call (hyperlink to Contact Us) if you should have any questions or would like pricing.
